Must Visit Places in Qatar

Katara

The old name of Qatar, Katara is a cultural village located between the West Bay, and the half-moon towers of The Pearl residential neighbourhood. Katara is Dohaâ€™s go-to destination for art, culture and cuisine.

West Bay

The West Bay district in Doha is home to some renowned government offices and foreign embassies. It is recognized as one of the most luxurious regions in the city, as the locality is accommodated by diplomats.

Souq Waqif

If you're after something with a more authentic feel, as well as a great night out, Souq Waqif is the place to be. Souq Waqif means "standing market," in Arabic and is the Qatari capital's oldest souq and a major tourist destination on its own.

Al Hazm Mall

Al Hazm means "the higher grounds" Arabic, it is a landmark of pure luxury in the heart of Doha. It is home to the world's finest restaurants and cafes, select fashion labels, and memorable events.
